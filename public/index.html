What YogaDham Does

Welcome to Sri Sri Jagannath Sanskrutika Parishad – YOGADHAM

Transforming Lives Through Yoga Since 1994

Sri Sri Jagannath Sanskrutika Parishad (YOGADHAM), established in 1994, is a pioneering institution dedicated to promoting the timeless wisdom and scientific benefits of Yoga for holistic well-being.

Located at Gandhi Nagar, 3rd East Extension, Brahmapur, Ganjam District, Odisha, India, YOGADHAM has been serving society for over three decades with an unwavering commitment to health, harmony, and human excellence.

Over the years, YOGADHAM has positively touched and transformed the lives of lakhs of individuals through yoga education, wellness initiatives, and community development programs.

Our Vision

To create a healthier, happier, and more harmonious society by spreading the transformative power of Yoga and Indian cultural values.

Our Mission

  • Promote Yoga as a way of life.
  • Foster physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being.
  • Nurture responsible, value-based citizens.
  • Provide accessible and authentic yoga education.
  • Contribute towards a healthier nation and a peaceful world.
"Yoga for Health, Harmony, Happiness and Humanity."
